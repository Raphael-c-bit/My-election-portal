STEP-BY-STEP ELECROAL PROCESS

1. Access the Voting Platform:

Open the official school voting platform URL provided by your institution using a browser on your computer or smartphone.

2. Log in to the System:

  1. Enter your Student ID and Password in the login section.
  2. Click the Login button to access the platform.
  3. If your credentials are incorrect, you will see an error message. Recheck your details and try again.

3. View the Dashboard:

After a successful login, you will be redirected to your Dashboard, which contains:

  • Voting guidelines and instructions.
  • A list of all positions (posts) available for voting.
  • Details of candidates contesting for each position.

4. Read Candidate Profiles:

Under each post (e.g., President, Secretary, Treasurer), view the profiles of all the candidates. Review their names, photos, and manifestos to understand their goals and how they plan to serve.

5. Cast Your Vote:

  1. For each position, select your preferred candidate:
    • Click on the Vote button for the candidate of your choice under the respective post.
  2. A confirmation alert will pop up asking if you’re sure about your choice. Confirm to proceed.

6. Review and Submit Votes:

Once you’ve voted for all positions, you will see a confirmation message: “Thank you for voting!” Your vote for each position is locked and cannot be changed after submission.

7. Logout:

After completing your voting, click the Logout button to securely exit the platform. This ensures that no one else can access your session or vote on your behalf.

8. Wait for Results:

The votes will be tallied after the voting deadline. Results will be announced by the school administration according to the specified timeline.

Additional Notes:

  • Eligibility: Only registered students with valid credentials can access the platform.
  • Voting Deadline: Ensure you cast your vote before the voting window closes.
  • Confidentiality: Your vote is anonymous and cannot be traced back to you.
  • Support: If you face any issues, contact the school’s IT support or electoral committee.
